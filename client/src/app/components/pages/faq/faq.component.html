Frequently Asked Questions

What is a ROM?
A ROM is a Read Only Memory.

How do I play a ROM on my computer?
In order to play a ROM in your computer, you need an emulator.
I recommend Retroarch.

What are cookies, and why does this site use them?
Part 1:  Cookies are little bits of data that websites store in your browser. They could be used for a variety of reasons.
Part 2:  This website uses cookies to store your JWT (JSON Web Token) for authentication. The JWT is a Bearer token that the API uses to verify that you are actually logged-in and authorized to access data such as ROMs.
